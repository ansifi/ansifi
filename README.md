## Programme write-ups

*Client constraint → engineering response → outcome. Matches [résumé PDF](https://ansifi.github.io/Ansif_Resume.pdf).*

### Cross-border logistics — TLMS (Malaysia & Singapore) · *2019 – 2022*

**Bottleneck:** Field apps and core databases lost sync during high-frequency cross-border freight — deadlocks and backlog.

**Response:** Sevendyne pod re-architected the Laravel transaction backend with **async queue workers** — stable telemetry across web, desktop, and mobile; thousands of live shipments daily.

**Stack:** Laravel · Vue.js · MySQL · REST · queue workers.

---

### Custom CRM & payments — PayU APAC · *2019 – 2022*

**Bottleneck:** Wallet, merchant, and logistics channels had outgrown separate systems across Singapore and Indonesia.

**Response:** Multi-tenant CodeIgniter/PHP operations, payment adapters, Android/web QR commerce — staged rollout with backward compatibility for live APAC traffic.

**Stack:** PHP/CodeIgniter · Android · multi-tenant REST.

---

### Zoho Recruit AI automation — Germany · *2022 – 2024*

**Bottleneck:** Recruiters spent hours on manual Zoho steps — off-the-shelf configuration could not streamline enrichment and screening.

**Response:** Deep Zoho customization + **Python/OpenAI automation** with operator-visible controls and human review in the loop.

**Stack:** Zoho APIs · Python · OpenAI · SQL.

---

### Enterprise analytics — Germany · *2022 – 2024*

**Bottleneck:** Operations needed maintainable data services and reliable aggregation — not one-off BI exports.

**Response:** Angular + Spring Boot + Apache eCharts with Python pipeline extensions, schema design, and release hygiene for EU restaurant and operations programmes.

**Stack:** Angular · Spring Boot · Python · PostgreSQL/SQL · eCharts.

---

### UK automotive & industrial team · *2019 – 2024*

**Bottleneck:** UK automation partner needed a stable bench for multi-year OEM programmes without an in-country engineering ops desk.

**Response:** Sevendyne built and managed SolidWorks, PLC, and SCADA engineers on **BMW** and **Land Rover** workstreams under UK programme leadership — five-year team continuity.

---

### Desktop trading — Qt · *2016 – 2018*

**Bottleneck:** Trading desks need low-latency multi-window layouts without coupling market-data adapters into UI widgets.

**Response:** Qt/C++ and Wt operator surfaces with **adapter boundaries** — foundational Sevendyne Qt proof reused in later programmes.

**Stack:** C++ · Qt · Wt · market-data UI.
